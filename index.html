About Agriculture Development Marketing Co-operative Society Ltd. (ADMC)

The primary goals of "Cooperation to Prosperity" are to encourage cooperative based economic development, strengthen the nation's cooperative movement and expand its grassroots influence, and develop the necessary institutional, legal, and policy frameworks to support cooperatives in achieving their goals.

Agriculture Development Marketing Co-operative Society Ltd. (ADMC) was incorporated in June 2023, under Multi State Co-operative Society Act, 2002 (39 of 2002), as a agro-cooperative, multi-dimensional organization to disseminate eco-friendly Technologies and Solutions for Holistic and Sustainable Development of Agriculture, Horticulture, Green-manuring crops, Organic Farming/ Natural Farming, Ornamental & Landscaping, Herbal parks, Aromatic & Medicinal plants, Spices and Allied Agro-social sector including Agricultural Marketing.
